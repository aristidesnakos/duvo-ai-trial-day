Derive from source. Reconcile. Hand a human the claim.

Human-gated, idempotent, with line-math and evidence behind every euro.

Meadowvale Dairy · the credibility centerpiece

It proves there's no claim — instead of inventing one.

  • The claim Jenny abandoned: "the numbers wouldn't line up."
  • The contract quotes per case = 12 units. The agent normalizes the unit of measure before any comparison.
  • The honest answer: €0. No claim. Stop chasing it.
PO   500 cases × €18.00 = €9,000
↳ 500 × 12 = 6,000 units @ €1.50
INV 6,000 units × €1.50 = €9,000
Ties exactly → no discrepancy.

It did the conversion that defeated a human — and refused to fabricate money to look clever. That's why the other €4,628 is believable.

Guardrails

Smallest safe surface. One gated write.

✓7 tools, exactly one write. Six are read-only and deterministic; only submit_claim acts.
✓Human-gated. Refuses until a real approval is recorded — nothing is written on trust.
✓Idempotent & capped. Keyed on PO + claim type, capped to the derived €; re-run = "already submitted."
✓Transparent, not inferred. Plain arithmetic an auditor re-checks — no LLM near a number.
✓Trace + audit by run_id. Who approved what, end to end. No secrets logged.
✓No fabrication. Missing evidence → not-claimable. The dairy €0 is the proof.
