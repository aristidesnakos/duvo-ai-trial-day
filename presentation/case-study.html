  1. Problem

    A ~€180M-GMV grocer is owed money by suppliers — short/damaged deliveries, over-contract prices, unclaimed rebates & promo — but claims lived in one person's spreadsheet, so what was never logged was never recovered.

  2. Symptoms

    No answer to "recovering vs. owed?" · claims reactive ("I catch what I catch") · rebate thresholds almost never checked · two weeks dark when the owner was out sick.

  3. Approach — shipped, not prototyped

    The agent buckets every claim missed / logged-correct / over-claimed / not-claimable and hands off a human-gated pack. Live on Duvo as a two-agent closed loop over a Sheets surrogate ERP — the supplier side answers from its own ledger, so credits and disputes are data-driven, not scripted.

  4. Proof — before / after

    Before: € owed unknown, recovery unanswerable. After: €6,203 owed · €4,628 missed · €450 duplicate caught · €29,200 double-billing blocked → €35,403 protected. The live loop drove recovery 0% → 89.2% with one approval.

  5. Trustworthy

    It refuses to fabricate — the dairy "hero" nets to €0 once units normalize, Sweet Treats stays unclaimable (no goods receipt), only the one rebate supplier over threshold is claimed. It shows where money isn't as confidently as where it is.
