A one-person spreadsheet — money walking out both doors.

  • Every claim is a manual PO ↔ Goods Receipt ↔ Invoice match across four files — reactive, slow, owned by one person.
  • Money never claimed (short / damaged / rebates) and money overpaid (duplicate bills) both slip through. "I catch what I catch."
  • Finance can't answer Mark — "how much are we recovering vs. owed?" — the recovery rate was never computed.

Tedious, error-prone, and it stops the moment Jenny is out.

What we automated · one safe agent

It does the whole tedious match — end to end.

One agent derives the claims that should exist — straight from PO ↔ GRN ↔ Invoice + contracts, not the spreadsheet — then reconciles them against the tracker, catching the inverse too: duplicate invoices we should never pay. Finally it hands off a human-gated claim with line-math and evidence behind every euro.
