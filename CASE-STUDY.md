# Case Study — Daily Basket Supplier Claims Agent

> Duvo internal-publish style: one headline + 5 bullets. Every number is live engine output, cross-checked against the run audit.

**Headline:** Daily Basket is owed money by its suppliers — they just couldn't say how much. Now they can: **€6,203 owed, €4,628 of it never logged, and €29,200 of double-billing stopped** — every euro backed by line-math a Finance Director can hold up in front of a supplier.

- **Problem:** Suppliers owe Daily Basket money all the time — short and damaged deliveries, prices above the agreed list, rebates and promo funding earned but never claimed. For a ~€180M online grocer on thin margins, that adds up. But it all lived in one spreadsheet, kept by one person (Jenny), so money nobody logged was money nobody recovered.
- **Symptoms:** When Mark, the Finance Director, asked *"how much are we recovering vs. how much are we owed?"*, Finance had no answer. Claims were caught by hand (*"I catch what I catch"*), rebate thresholds on flour, drinks, packaging and dairy went unchecked, and the two weeks Jenny was out sick the whole process stopped — with credit windows about to close.
- **Approach — shipped, not prototyped:** Rather than digitize the spreadsheet, the agent works out the claims that *should* exist straight from the source — purchase orders, goods receipts, invoices and contracts — and sorts each one into **missed / logged-correct / over-claimed / not-claimable**, with a human approving before anything leaves the building. It's **live on Duvo** as a two-agent closed loop over a Google Sheets surrogate ERP, and it runs whether or not Jenny is in. Deployment record: `aop/DEPLOYMENT.md`.
- **Proof — before/after (€):** before, the number owed was *unknown* and the recovery rate *unanswerable*. After: **€6,203 owed · €4,628 never logged** (Riverside damage €1,080 + Northgate rebate €1,548 + Sunrise promo €2,000 — the promo *medium-confidence*, as that contract lapses mid-quarter) · **€450 duplicate** caught (Prime Cuts, logged twice) · **€29,200 of double-billing stopped "DO NOT PAY"** before AP paid it → **€35,403 protected** in one quarter (annualized ≈ €24,812/yr). Recovered so far: **€0** — the claims are found and packaged, not yet collected; we ran the full recovery loop end-to-end on Duvo as a live demonstration. 18/18 acceptance criteria pass.
- **What earns trust:** it won't invent money. The dairy claim Jenny could never get to balance comes out to **€0** once the per-case units are converted — so they can stop chasing it. A shortage with no goods receipt is left **unclaimable** rather than guessed. Only the one supplier that genuinely crossed its rebate threshold is claimed, and a tracker row pointing at an invoice that doesn't exist is flagged **"reference not found."** It's as clear about where the money *isn't* as where it is.
